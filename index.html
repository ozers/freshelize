Privacy Policy for Freshelize

Last updated: 11.04.2025

1. Data Storage

Freshelize stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or process any of your data on our servers.

2. Optional iCloud Sync

You have the option to enable iCloud sync, which will store your data in your personal iCloud account. This is completely optional and controlled by you.

When iCloud sync is enabled, your data is subject to Apple's privacy policies and terms of service.

3. Data We Never Collect

We do not collect or store:

  • Personal information
  • Usage statistics
  • Analytics data
  • Any other user data

4. Your Control

You have full control over your data:

  • All data is stored only on your device
  • You can delete all data at any time
  • You can enable/disable iCloud sync as you wish

5. Contact

If you have any questions about your privacy while using Freshelize, please contact us at ozersubasi.dev@gmail.com
