Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my data across devices?

Go to Settings > Enable iCloud Sync to keep your data synchronized across your Apple devices.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete all your data by going to Settings > Clear All Data.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or store any of your information on our servers.
